iCons Installation Guide
    • Choose the app icon you want to theme.
    • Select a theme.
    • You'll be redirected to the app.
    • Open Safari back and you'll see a white page.
    • Add that page to your Homescreen by pressing the Box with an up arrow in it
    • Enjoy your new themed icon!
    • Come back here to install new themed icons!;)
